About Us
Before 2012 Enactus was known as Students In Free Enterprises (SIFE). SIFE is an international non profit organization which consists of students, teachers and business leaders who tender to the economical needs of the society’s lower strata through an entrepreneurial approach. Since it’s founding in 1975 it has been one of the most unique international organization with more than 66,000 members contributing from 38 different countries . Participating Students join teams on their university campuses and undertake business projects which cater to improve the quality of life and standard of living for people in need. In addition to improving the community, the members of SIFE constantly aim to become individuals of ethics, business and humanity. Sife was renamed to Enactus to imply on the uniqueness of the organization.


Founded in 1975, the Enactus organization began in the United States. Today, Enactus India operates as a subsidiary of Enactus Worldwide and is one of 36 countries around the world that comprises Enactus’s premier business and higher education network. As one of the largest Enactus country operations, Enactus India is a priority country for the future growth of the organization. Enactus India works with leaders in business and higher education to mobilize university students to make a difference in their communities while developing their skills to become socially responsible business leaders.
