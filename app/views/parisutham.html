Parisutham


A project which deals with the manufacturing of cleaning aids in specific phenyl . It seeks to address the unhygienic lifestyle by promoting cleanliness . A section of Tsunami victims were used to manufacture the products inorder to stabilize their lives again in terms of money. Parisutham a Tamil word meaning cleanliness, is symbolic of the vision of the project. Cleanse the past and redefine the future. Phenyl is a common utility in every household. For these women it was the trump card to success.
